## E12 — confirmed-capitulation MR ("right side of the V"): **FAIL**

Arm on ≥15% drop off the 10-day high + RVOL ≥ 1.5 climax; enter on first
close above the prior day's high; trail on prior-bar lows; 40-bar max hold.

| Window | CAGR | maxDD | Sharpe | n | win |

| **Gate 2000–2013** | **−4.71%** | 55.0% | −0.41 | 90 | 35.6% |

overnight-gap arithmetic that killed the IBS family — and the prior-bar-low
trail then whipsaws out in the post-capitulation chop. Confirmed-capitulation
closed.

**The honest nuance:** E10 is the *only* experiment in the entire program to
beat both benchmarks in the hostile regime — +5.93%/yr through 2000–2013
while its own universe lost money and SPY made 1.72%. Even allowing for
survivorship flattery, PEAD showed a real risk-adjusted signature there. But
post-2014 the drift underperforms both benchmarks — consistent with the
academic record that PEAD decayed after ~2010 as it was arbitraged away. As a
high-return strategy it fails decisively in every window, and the FAIL is
interpretable precisely because the biases could only have helped. PEAD is
closed for backtested high-return claims; the 2000-13 signature is noted as
the program's one "real but small and decayed" effect.
